Diabetes Education

Understanding Blood Sugar Levels

Normal ranges:

  • Fasting: 70-100 mg/dL (non-diabetic), 80-130 mg/dL (diabetic target)
  • After meals (2 hours): Less than 140 mg/dL (non-diabetic), Less than 180 mg/dL (diabetic target)
  • A1C: Below 5.7% (normal), 5.7-6.4% (prediabetes), 6.5%+ (diabetes)

Carb Counting Basics

Carbohydrates have the biggest impact on blood sugar. Common sources:

  • Grains: Bread (15g per slice), Rice (45g per cup), Pasta (30-45g per cup)
  • Fruits: Banana (30g), Apple (25g), Orange (15g)
  • Dairy: Milk (12g per cup), Yogurt (15-30g per cup)
  • Vegetables: Most non-starchy veggies have minimal carbs

Lifestyle Tips

  • Stay physically active - aim for 150 minutes of moderate exercise per week
  • Monitor your blood sugar regularly as recommended by your doctor
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Stay hydrated with water
  • Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)
  • Manage stress through meditation, yoga, or other relaxation techniques
  • Take medications as prescribed
  • Regular checkups with your healthcare team
